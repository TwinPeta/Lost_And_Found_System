ABOUT US

The Makerere University Business School Lost and Found System is a web application that helps students of Makerere University Business School find and return lost items. The system is developed in Django Python and is available to use 24/7. The system was created by a team of students at Makerere University Business School who are passionate about making the university a more convenient and enjoyable place to study. The team believes that the lost and found system will help to reduce the number of lost items on campus and make it easier for people to find their belongings. The system is easy to use. To report a lost item, simply create a new listing and provide a description of the item, the location where it was lost, and your contact information. To find a lost item, simply browse the listings or search by keyword. The system is also secure. All personal information is stored confidentially and only accessible to authorized personnel. The Makerere University Business School Lost and Found System is a valuable resource for the university community. It is a convenient way to find and return lost items, and it helps to make the university a more welcoming and enjoyable place to be. If you have any questions about the system, please contact the team at mubs.ac.ug
